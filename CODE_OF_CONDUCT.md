# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors and maintainers to act professionally and respectfully.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus discussion on the code, product, and facts.
- Assume good intent unless there is clear reason not to.
- Accept technical disagreement without personal attacks.
- Keep feedback actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks or insults
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith escalation
- publishing private information without permission

## Moderation

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If conduct becomes a project problem, contact the maintainers privately rather than escalating publicly.
